ABOUT ME

Ride with Faye

Faye is a visionary snowboarding enthusiast with a profound passion for the sport. With years of experience carving the slopes and a deep appreciation for the mountains, Faye envisioned creating a snowboarding school that would provide a transformative experience for every student. As a certified snowboarding instructor, Faye brings a wealth of expertise, leadership, and a commitment to fostering a welcoming community within the snowboarding world.

What are we offering?

Photography Service

We offer professional photography services, capturing exhilarating moments on the slopes. Continuous feedback and flexibility ensure exceptional service.

Videography Service

Our skilled team captures every exhilarating moment on the slopes, turning your snowboarding adventures into cinematic masterpieces. From breathtaking jumps and heart-pounding descents to stylish tricks and awe-inspiring scenery, we ensure that every epic move and scenic view is beautifully documented.

Tailored Snowboarding Experiences

Our instructors combine their extensive snowboarding expertise with a passion for teaching, ensuring that you receive focused guidance and support. Through one-on-one sessions or small group settings, we break down complex maneuvers, provide real-time feedback, and help you master essential skills, from carving smooth turns to nailing challenging tricks.
